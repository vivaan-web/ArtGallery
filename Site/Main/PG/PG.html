Password Creation Guidelines

I ensure your security at Vivaanwiz.com, but passwords are your choice and I would like to guide you on creating them. Here are the characteristics of a strong password:

  • Has 8-12 characters or more.
    (More characters, the strong it is)
  • Is comprised of:

      # Characters
      1 Lowercase letters (a-z)
      2 Uppercase letters (A-Z)
      3 Numbers (0-9)
      4 Special Characters:
      `~!@#$%^&*()-_=+[]\{}|;':",./<>?
Google has a secure password generator that works on many sites All you need is Google Chrome browser and a google account. This feature is likely to be added to other browsers too, so keep an eye on it.

Why should a password be secure?

The reason secure passwords are important is because of data integrity and security. If you had confidential information on your computer and someone knew the pin or password, they cen get hold of information they aren't supposed to have. Getting hacked is a big problem, especially with social media. If someone is in your social media account and is impersonating you and acting all strange. But the biggest problem is with financial passwords. If people get in to your bank account, they can use your money without your permission, that is also why you don't reply to spam and use sketchy websites. PI

What is PI? PI is personal information. On the internet, keep away from sharing your PI to others, PI is anything that can be used to identify you and that is why you shouldn't share it with others. Why is sharing PI dangerous? A number of reasons!
  • identity theft
  • Spam
  • Phishing
The weird thing is you don't even need a social media or network account to be subject to sharing PI. If you do have an account, you don't even have to be online!
